# Incident + Privacy Playbook (v0.9)

## Severity levels
Sev0 (Critical):
- widespread outage or data loss risk
Targets:
- triage within 10 minutes
- customer update within 30 minutes
- mitigation within 2 hours (best effort)

Sev1 (Major):
Targets:
- triage within 30 minutes
- customer update within 2 hours

Sev2 (Minor):
Targets:
- triage within 1 business day

## Customer communications cadence
- Sev0: updates every 30 minutes until mitigated
- Sev1: updates every 2 hours until mitigated

## Data subject requests (DSRs)
We aim to respond within 25 days of receiving a request.

## Legal holds
If a legal hold is active:
- deletion is paused for affected data
- we confirm hold status in the DSR response

## Handling Restricted data during incidents
Do not paste Restricted data into chat tools.
If you must reference Restricted data, use an approved ticket marked “Restricted” with access limited.
